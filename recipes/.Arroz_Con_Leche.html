Arroz con leche

Learn how to prepare a delicius spanish dessert

Ingredients:

  • 1l milk
  • 20gr rice
  • 1 branch cinnamon
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 orange
  • 5gr sugar

Instructions:

Just follow this easy steps

  1. Heat the milk
  2. when milk is hot add the rice
  3. add the lemon and orange
  4. add the cinnamon
  5. let it cook in low heat for 45 minutes
  6. Remove from fire and add the sugar
  7. Now let it cool of, when its cool it will be ready to enjoy!
