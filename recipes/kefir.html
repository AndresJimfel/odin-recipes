Kefir

Now lets see how we prepare some Kefir

Ingredients

  • 1l milk
  • 2gr Kefir fungus

Instructions

Now lets prepare Kefir

  1. Let the milk warm up to room temperature
  2. Add the fungus to a 1l bottle
  3. Pour the milk inside the bottle
  4. Stir the whole thing
  5. Let the fungus ferment 48h and it will be ready to drink
